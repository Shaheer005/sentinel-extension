# 🛡️ Sentinel — Gmail Phishing Defender

Sentinel silently protects your Gmail inbox from phishing attacks 
before you even open an email.

## What it does
The moment an email arrives, Sentinel scans it automatically and 
shows a safety badge right in your inbox — no clicks needed.

- 🔴 **PHISHING** — dangerous email detected
- 🟡 **SUSPICIOUS** — something looks off
- 🟢 **Safe** — looks clean

## What it detects
- Tracking pixels spying on you
- Spoofed display names (PayPal → paypa1.ru)
- Lookalike domains (amaz0n.com, g00gle.net)
- Malicious redirect links
- Credential harvesting forms
- Urgency language & fake deadlines
- Reply-To hijacking
- Hidden text spam bypass
- Suspicious attachments (.exe, .bat, .ps1)
- Newly registered domains

## Privacy
All scanning happens **locally in your browser**.  
Your emails never leave your device. No account required.

## Install
Available on the **Microsoft Edge Add-ons Store** — search "Sentinel Phishing Defender"
